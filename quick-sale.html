Why Traditional Sales Are Too Slow

The typical Winnipeg home sale timeline:

Traditional Listing = 3-6 Months Minimum

  • Week 1-2: Find realtor, sign listing agreement, stage home, photos

  • Week 9-12: Receive offers, negotiate, accept offer
  • Week 13-16: Buyer inspection, financing approval, appraisal
