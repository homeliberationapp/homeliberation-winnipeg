## ğŸ¯ OFF-MARKET APARTMENT TARGETING SYSTEM

### **WHY OWNERS SELL OFF-MARKET:**

1. **Don't want tenants to know** (causes panic, move-outs)
2. **Privacy** (wealthy owners, family offices)
3. **Avoid market stigma** (if building has issues)
4. **Speed** (need quick liquidity)
5. **Avoid broker fees** (6% on $2M = $120k saved)
6. **Testing market** (see what offers come in)
7. **1031 exchange timing** (need to close in 45 days)

### **FINDING OFF-MARKET DEALS:**

**Method 1: Property Tax Records (Public Data)**

Access Winnipeg Assessment & Taxation:
- https://www.winnipegassessment.com/
- Search by address, owner name, or assessment roll

Target criteria:
- Multi-family (3+ units)
- Owner for 10+ years (likely older, tired)
- Out-of-province owner address (absentee)
- High assessed value increase (property appreciated, time to cash out)
- Corporate ownership ending in "Ltd" or "Inc" (may be selling)

Pull data:
- Owner name
- Mailing address
- Property details
- Assessment value
- Tax status

Create direct mail list:
500-1,000 owners meeting criteria

**Method 2: Corporate Registry Search**

Manitoba Companies Office:
- Search for numbered companies (123456 Manitoba Ltd)
- Many apartments owned through corps
- Registry shows directors/officers (actual owners)
- Some corps formed 20-40 years ago = aging owners

Target:
- Old corporations (formed 1970s-1990s)
- Directors age 65+ (ready to retire)
- Single-director corps (no succession plan)

- Public record of deceased persons' estates
