Investor FAQ

How do you source these deals? +
We work directly with motivated property owners who need to sell quickly. We get properties under contract, then offer them to our investor network through assignment contracts.
What's included in the data pack? +
Every deal includes: property details (bed/bath/sqft), 3 verified comps, ARV calculation, estimated repairs, photos, neighborhood analysis, assignment fee breakdown, and seller motivation level.
How does the assignment process work? +
When you win a deal, we assign our purchase contract to you. You pay the assignment fee (disclosed upfront), then close directly with the seller using your preferred title company. We handle all paperwork via DocuSign.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, cancel anytime. If you're on the Investor or Pro tier and cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing cycle.
Do I need to be an accredited investor? +
No. You need to demonstrate you're a qualified real estate investor (proof of funds, past deals, or lender pre-approval). We verify this during signup to maintain quality in our buyer network.
