We Handle Complex Problems

Don't see your situation listed on our main pages? No problem. We've successfully helped homeowners facing all kinds of unique, complicated, and difficult property situations. Here are just a few examples.

Hoarder Houses

Property filled with belongings? Years of accumulation? Overwhelming cleanup? We buy hoarder houses as-is and handle all cleanout. You don't touch a thing.

Condemned Properties

City issued a condemnation notice? Code violations? Unsafe to occupy? We purchase condemned properties and deal with the city on your behalf.

Grow-Op Houses

Former marijuana grow operation? Mold issues? Electrical problems? Stigmatized property? We buy these and handle all remediation.

Crime Scene Properties

Unfortunate incident occurred? Stigmatized property? Difficult to sell traditionally? We handle these sensitive situations with discretion and respect.

Squatter Situations

Unauthorized occupants? Legal eviction nightmares? We can help navigate these complex situations and get the property transferred.

Title Issues

Clouded title? Liens? Ownership disputes? Missing heirs? We work with real estate lawyers to resolve title problems.

Structural Damage

Major foundation issues? Structural compromise? Sinking foundation? Collapsed roof? We buy properties with severe structural problems.

Environmental Issues

Asbestos? Lead paint? Oil tank leaks? Contaminated soil? We handle properties with environmental hazards.

Legal Judgments

Property liens from lawsuits? Court-ordered sales? Creditor issues? We can work with you to satisfy judgments through property sale.

IRS Tax Liens

Federal or provincial tax liens on your property? We coordinate with authorities to settle liens through the sale process.

Multiple Owner Disputes

Co-owners can't agree? Family disputes over inherited property? Partnership dissolution? We help navigate multi-party situations.

Reverse Mortgage Issues

Inherited a property with a reverse mortgage? Struggling to satisfy the loan? We can help you resolve these situations quickly.

Mental Health / Capacity Issues

Selling on behalf of someone with diminished capacity? Power of attorney situations? We work with families and lawyers sensitively.

Incarceration Situations

Need to sell while serving time? Can't maintain the property? We handle these discreetly and help you move forward.

Immigration / Deportation

Leaving Canada? Need to liquidate assets quickly? We can help you sell fast before you leave.

Natural Disaster Damage

Flood damage? Storm damage? Tree fell through the house? Insurance won't cover it all? We buy disaster-damaged properties.

The Common Thread

What all these situations have in common is that traditional real estate doesn't work. Realtors can't list these properties. Banks won't finance them. Regular buyers won't touch them.

That's where we come in. We're problem solvers, not just home buyers. We find solutions where others see dead ends.
