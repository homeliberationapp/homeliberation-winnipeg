    Property to Buyer (or Buyer's Assignee) as per this Agreement.

    any defense that monetary damages would be adequate.

(b) LIQUIDATED DAMAGES: $[15,000-25,000] payable to Buyer immediately
    upon Seller's breach, as reasonable pre-estimate of Buyer's
    damages including lost assignment fees, opportunity costs, and
    expenses incurred.

(c) ACTUAL DAMAGES: In addition to or instead of (a) or (b), Buyer
    may claim actual damages including but not limited to:
    - Lost assignment fees
    - Lost profits on assignment
    - Legal fees and costs
    - Expenses incurred in reliance on this Agreement
    - Consequential damages
