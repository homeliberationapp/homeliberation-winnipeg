Find Comps (Winnipeg)

Use these tools to find comparable sales:

Free Resources:

  • Realtor.ca - Search sold listings in Winnipeg
  • Winnipeg Assessment - Property tax assessments
  • Google: "recently sold homes [neighborhood] Winnipeg"
  • Drive neighborhood: Look for sold signs, note addresses

Comp Analysis Formula:

1. Find 3-5 sold homes within 0.5km
2. Similar size (췀20% sqft)
3. Sold within last 6 months
4. Calculate $/sqft average
5. Adjust for condition differences
6. That's your ARV baseline
