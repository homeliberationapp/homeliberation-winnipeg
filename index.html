Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you help me?
We respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. If it's a fit, we can move as quickly or slowly as you need. Some situations close in 7-14 days, others take a few weeks. We work on your timeline.
Do I need to make repairs?
No. We buy properties in any condition‚Äîfoundation issues, roof damage, fire damage, hoarding situations, code violations, you name it. You don't fix a single thing.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero fees. No commissions. No closing costs. No hidden charges. What we discuss is what you get.
What if I have tenants?
We buy tenant-occupied properties all the time. You don't need to evict anyone. We handle the transition and take over tenant relationships.
Is this confidential?
Completely. We understand many situations are sensitive‚Äîdivorce, foreclosure, financial stress. Everything stays private. Your neighbors don't need to know.
What types of properties do you buy?
Single-family homes, duplexes, triplexes, apartment buildings, multi-family units, mixed-use buildings, vacant land with structures, property portfolios‚Äîif it's in Winnipeg, we're interested.
How do you determine what to offer?
We analyze comparable sales in your neighborhood, assess property condition, factor in repair costs, consider current market conditions, and structure an offer that works for your situation. Every property is unique.
What if I owe more than the house is worth?
We work with underwater properties. We can negotiate with lenders, explore short sales, or find creative solutions. We've helped homeowners in negative equity situations before.
Can you help with commercial or investment properties?
Yes. We buy apartment buildings, commercial properties, mixed-use buildings, and entire investment portfolios. If you're a landlord looking to exit multiple properties, we can help with that too.
Do I need to clean out the property?
No. Leave everything. Furniture, belongings, junk in the basement‚Äîwe handle all cleanout. You take what you want and leave the rest.
What makes you different from realtors or iBuyers?
Realtors need the property listed for months and market-ready. iBuyers only want near-perfect homes. We buy problem properties that others won't touch‚Äîforeclosures, major repairs, tenant issues, code violations. We solve problems, not just buy houses.
Can I get multiple offers or shop around?
Absolutely. We encourage you to explore all options. If someone offers better terms that work for your situation, take it. We're here to help, not pressure you.
What if the property is in another city or province?
We focus on Winnipeg and surrounding areas, but we have a network for out-of-province properties. Contact us and we'll see if we can help or refer you to someone who can.
