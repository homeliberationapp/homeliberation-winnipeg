How fast can you buy my house?
We respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. If it's a fit, we can close in as little as 2-4 weeks for urgent situations, or we can work on your timeline if you need 2-4 weeks. We move as quickly or slowly as you need.
Do I need to make any repairs?
Absolutely not. We buy homes in any condition‚Äîfoundation cracks, roof leaks, fire damage, hoarding situations, code violations, mold, you name it. You don't fix a single thing. We handle all repairs after we buy.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero. No realtor commissions (typically 5-6%). No closing costs. No inspection fees. No hidden charges. What we discuss is exactly what you get.
How do you determine your offer price?
We analyze comparable sales in your neighborhood, assess the property condition, factor in repair costs, consider current Winnipeg market conditions, and create a fair offer. Every property is unique, so we tailor our offers to your specific situation.
What if I have tenants in the property?
We buy tenant-occupied properties all the time. You don't need to evict anyone or wait for leases to expire. We take over the property with tenants in place and handle the landlord relationship from there.
Is this process confidential?
100% confidential. We understand situations like foreclosure, divorce, and financial stress are sensitive. We don't advertise the property, put up signs, or tell neighbors. Everything stays completely private.
What types of properties do you buy?
Single-family homes, duplexes, triplexes, fourplexes, apartment buildings, multi-family units, mixed-use commercial/residential buildings, rental property portfolios, and land with structures. If it's in Winnipeg, we're interested.
What if I owe more than the house is worth?
We work with underwater properties regularly. We can negotiate with your lender, explore short sale options, or find creative solutions to help you move forward without owing money after the sale.
Can you help with inherited properties?
Yes. We handle probate sales regularly. We work with executors and estate lawyers, coordinate with out-of-province heirs, handle property cleanouts, and simplify the entire process.

No. Leave everything‚Äîfurniture, belongings, junk in the basement, garbage, everything. Take what you want and leave the rest. We handle all cleanout, disposal, and hauling.
What makes you different from realtors?
Realtors list your property for months and require it to be market-ready (repairs, staging, showings). We buy directly in as-is condition and close in days, not months. No repairs, no showings, no waiting for buyers.

iBuyers only want near-perfect homes in certain neighborhoods and often back out after inspection. We buy problem properties‚Äîforeclosures, fire damage, foundation issues, tenant problems, code violations‚Äîproperties that iBuyers and traditional buyers won't touch.
Can I get multiple offers or shop around?
Absolutely. We encourage you to explore all options and choose what's best for you. If someone offers better terms, take it. We're here to help, not pressure you.

That's what we specialize in. Fire damage, flood damage, foundation cracks, roof collapse, hoarding situations, code violations, mold, asbestos‚Äîwe've seen it all and bought it all. Bad condition doesn't scare us.
Can you buy commercial or investment properties?
Yes. We purchase apartment buildings, commercial properties, mixed-use buildings, and entire real estate investment portfolios. If you're a landlord looking to exit multiple properties, we can help with that too.

Yes. We work with homeowners facing bankruptcy, foreclosure, tax liens, and financial crises regularly. We understand the urgency and can move quickly to help you avoid foreclosure or bankruptcy complications.
