Foreclosure is terrifying. The threatening letters, the legal notices, the sleepless nights wondering if you'll lose your home. The shame of watching your neighbors see the bank's notices on your door.

Many Winnipeg homeowners face foreclosure due to circumstances beyond their control:

  • Job Loss: Lost your income and can't keep up with mortgage payments
  • Medical Emergency: Overwhelming medical bills draining your savings
  • Divorce: Separation leaving you unable to afford the house alone
  • Business Failure: Self-employed income disappeared
  • Adjustable Rate Increase: Your mortgage payment jumped and you can't afford it
  • Property Damage: Uninsured damage making the home unlivable
  • Tax Arrears: Behind on property taxes and the city is threatening action

Foreclosure destroys your credit for 7 years. It makes renting difficult, raises insurance rates, and can even impact job prospects. But there's a better way.

How We Help You Avoid Foreclosure

Fast Cash Purchase

We buy your home quickly for cash, paying off the mortgage before foreclosure completes. You walk away with your credit intact and potentially cash in your pocket.

Subject-To Acquisition

We take over your mortgage payments immediately, stopping foreclosure proceedings. You're released from liability while we assume the debt and property.

Short Sale Assistance

If you owe more than the home is worth, we negotiate with your lender for a short sale, avoiding foreclosure and minimizing credit damage.

Deed in Lieu

We work with you and the bank to transfer the property voluntarily, avoiding foreclosure while protecting your credit as much as possible.
