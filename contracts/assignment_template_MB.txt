3.1 The purchase price for the Property as agreed with Seller is $[PURCHASE PRICE] CAD.

3.2 Assignee shall pay the purchase price directly to Seller at closing.

3.3 Option fee of $500 CAD paid by Assignor to Seller shall be credited to purchase price.

4.1 Assignee shall deposit $[DEPOSIT AMOUNT] CAD into escrow with [TITLE COMPANY/LAWYER]

4.2 The deposit shall be held in trust and applied to the purchase price at closing.

4.3 If the transaction does not close due to Seller default, the deposit shall be refunded to Assignee.

4.4 If the transaction does not close due to Assignee default (after Due Diligence Period),
the deposit shall be forfeited.

5. DUE DILIGENCE PERIOD

5.1 Assignee shall have [10] calendar days from the date of this Agreement (the "Due Diligence Period")
to conduct the following:
    a) Property inspection (structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing)
    b) Title search and review
    c) Survey review
    d) Zoning verification
    e) Environmental assessment (if applicable)
    f) Financing arrangement (if applicable)

5.2 If Assignee is not satisfied for ANY reason within the Due Diligence Period, Assignee may
terminate this Agreement by written notice to Assignor on or before [DUE DILIGENCE END DATE].

5.3 Upon termination during Due Diligence Period:
    - Earnest deposit shall be refunded to Assignee in full
    - Assignment Fee shall be refunded to Assignee in full
    - All parties shall be released from further obligation

5.4 If Assignee does not terminate by the end of the Due Diligence Period, this Agreement becomes
firm and binding with no further conditions.

    c) Legal fees, title insurance, and closing costs as applicable

6.5 Possession of the Property shall be transferred to Assignee on the closing date.
